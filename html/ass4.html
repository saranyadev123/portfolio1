    2. SONG

    "Cheri, Cheri Lady" is a song by German pop duo Modern Talking from their second studio album, Let's Talk About Love (1985). It was released as the album's only single on 2 September 1985[2] and reached number one on the German Singles Chart, becoming the duo's third consecutive number-one single in Germany, following "You're My Heart, You're My Soul" and "You Can Win If You Want". The single spent four weeks at the top and a total of 24 weeks on the German chart,[2] and eventually went gold, selling over 250,000 units there.[3] "Cheri, Cheri Lady" has also been certified silver inFrance, denoting sales in excess of 250,000 units.[4]
